Privacy Policy

This is based off of the Reddit Privacy Policy structure. I have no idea what I'm doing, but I think this should work. Comments? Email me.

What We Collect

We collect…

  1. If you choose to create an account, you give us a site username and password.
  2. The content you enter into the site, e.g.:
    1. uploaded paste content
    2. paste titles
    3. Miscellaneous paste data such as expiry time and programming language.
  3. By the nature of IPv4/IPv6, you also share your public facing internet address with us.

How Long is Data Retained?

IP addresses are retained only in logs, which are flushed occasionally.

All other data is retained as long as you have an account on this service. Please see Your Choices for information on account removal.

How We Use Information About You

All data (except for IP addresses and users' passwords) is used to build various pages on the website.

How Information About You is Shared

We do not share data with other organizations or websites.

Ads and Analytics Partners

We have no ads or analytics partners.

Your Choices

If you wish for your data to be removed from the site, please send an email stating your username and that you wish for your site data to be removed.

Contact Us

Write us any time at sillypaste-app@winny.tech with inquiries and account removal requests.
